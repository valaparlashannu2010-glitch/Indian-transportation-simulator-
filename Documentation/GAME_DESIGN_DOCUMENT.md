# Indian Bus Simulator - Complete Game Design Document

## Executive Summary

**Title**: Indian Bus Simulator - Complete Transportation Ecosystem
**Genre**: Simulation / Transportation / Sandbox
**Platform**: iOS & Android (Native Performance)
**Target Audience**: Ages 10+, Casual to Core Gamers
**USP**: First hyper-realistic Indian transportation simulator with all vehicles, complete passenger system, and all-India geography

---

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. **Select Vehicle** → Choose bus, car, bike, truck, or auto
2. **Select Route** → Pick destination from available routes
3. **Drive & Experience** → Navigate traffic, passengers, weather
4. **Complete Journey** → Reach destination, earn revenue
5. **Get Feedback** → Passenger ratings and reviews
6. **Earn Rewards** → Unlock vehicles, customizations, progression

## Passenger System - Complete Ecosystem

### Passenger Types & Behaviors

**1. Office Workers**
- Travel times: 7-9 AM, 5-7 PM
- Preferences: Window seats, direct routes
- Satisfaction factors: Punctuality, comfort
- Usually carry small bags

**2. Students**
- Travel times: 7-8 AM, 2-4 PM
- Preferences: Affordable, school/college routes
- Satisfaction factors: Safety, reliability
- Group behavior (travel together)

**3. Senior Citizens**
- Travel times: Off-peak hours
- Preferences: Accessible seating near entrance
- Satisfaction factors: Safety, comfort, respectful behavior
- May need assistance boarding
- Get senior discounts

**4. Mothers with Children**
- Travel times: Morning, afternoon
- Preferences: Family seating together
- Satisfaction factors: Safety, smooth driving
- Children get active during journey
- Stroller/luggage accommodation

**5. Manual Workers**
- Travel times: Early morning, evening
- Preferences: Affordable routes
- Satisfaction factors: Reliability, cost
- Heavy luggage (tools, materials)
- Sit in back seats usually

**6. Tourists**
- Travel times: Throughout day
- Preferences: Long-distance, scenic routes
- Satisfaction factors: Comfort, experience
- Usually have cameras and luggage
- May not speak local language

**7. Business Travelers**
- Travel times: Any time
- Preferences: Premium buses, comfortable
- Satisfaction factors: Punctuality, premium service
- Business attire, laptops/files
- Higher fare paying

**8. Occasional Travelers**
- Peak hours, long-distance
- Variable preferences
- Satisfaction factors: Cost, directness

### Boarding & Alighting System

**Boarding Process**:
1. Passengers wait at bus stop
2. Bus arrives and stops
3. Passengers check destination with conductor/app
4. They board through designated doors
5. Animation shows boarding with luggage
6. They find seats or standing space
7. Luggage placed in overhead racks or feet

**Seating Dynamics**:
- Window seats preferred
- Aisle seats as overflow
- Front seats for elderly
- Family groups sit together
- Strangers avoid middle seats initially

**Alighting**:
- Passengers press bell or call out
- Stand up and move to doors
- Collect luggage
- Exit with animation
- Realistic crowding dynamics

### Passenger Satisfaction System

**Rating Categories** (Each 1-5 stars):

1. **Comfort Rating** (40% weight)
   - Smooth acceleration/braking
   - Suspension comfort
   - Seating quality
   - Temperature (AC/heating)
   - Crowding level

2. **Safety Rating** (25% weight)
   - Smooth cornering
   - No rash speed changes
   - Defensive driving
   - No near-misses
   - Professional handling

3. **Cleanliness Rating** (15% weight)
   - Vehicle cleanliness
   - Maintenance condition
   - Interior tidiness
   - Hygiene standards

4. **Punctuality Rating** (15% weight)
   - On-time arrival
   - Following schedule
   - Direct route
   - No unnecessary stops

5. **Driver Behavior Rating** (5% weight)
   - Politeness
   - Professionalism
   - Responsiveness
   - Language skill

### Revenue System

**Fare Structure**:
- Per kilometer base fare
- Distance multiplier
- Time of day surcharge (peak hours)
- AC premium (if applicable)
- Student/Senior discounts (-30%)
- Luggage charges
- Extra seat reservation

**Daily Revenue Calculation**:
```
Total Revenue = (Passengers × Average Fare) + (Luggage Charges) - (Operating Costs)

Operating Costs:
- Fuel consumption
- Maintenance
- Driver salary
- Insurance
- Toll fees
- Registration
```

**Financial Tracking**:
- Daily revenue report
- Monthly statements
- Route profitability
- Fleet efficiency
- Seasonal patterns

## Weather System - Regional Variations

### Monsoon (June-September)
- **Affected Regions**: Kerala, Western Ghats, Northeast, coastal areas
- **Features**: Heavy rainfall, flooding, reduced visibility, landslides
- **Handling**: Reduced grip, increased stopping distance, puddles
- **Audio**: Heavy rain sounds, thunder

### Heat Season (April-June)
- **Affected Regions**: Rajasthan, Central India, Northern plains
- **Features**: 45°C+ temperatures, dust storms, mirages
- **Handling**: Tire damage, engine overheating, reduced visibility
- **Audio**: Wind howling, silence (extreme heat)

### Winter (December-February)
- **Affected Regions**: Himalayas, Northern plains, higher altitudes
- **Features**: Snow coverage, icy roads, fog
- **Handling**: Very low grip, skidding possible, visibility issues
- **Audio**: Wind, silence, occasional vehicle sounds muffled

### Seasonal Transitions
- **Pre-Monsoon** (May-June): Dust, heat
- **Post-Monsoon** (September-October): Humidity, flooding aftermath
- **Dry Season** (November-March): Clear, dust
